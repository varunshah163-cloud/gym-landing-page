Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

What are the gym timings?

We're open 24/7 for Pro and Elite members. Basic members can access the gym from 6 AM to 10 PM, Monday through Sunday including holidays.

Is there a registration fee?

No! We don't charge any registration or joining fees. You only pay for your membership plan. We also offer a 3-day free trial for new members.

Can I freeze my membership if I travel?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 30 days per year at no extra cost. Just inform us 7 days in advance.

Do you offer personal training for beginners?

Absolutely! Our trainers specialize in working with beginners. We start with a free fitness assessment and create a plan that matches your current fitness level.
